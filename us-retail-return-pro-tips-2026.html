Avoid Common Mistakes

One of the biggest mistakes in 2026 is forgetting to check for **restocking fees**. Tech retailers are increasingly charging 10-15% fees for opened electronics. Always use our calculator before opening the seal.

Take a Photo

When returning via a drop-off point or mail, **always take a photo** of the item and the return shipping receipt. This is your only protection if the package is lost or the retailer claims it arrived empty.
