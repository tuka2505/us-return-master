Walmart's 2026 Refund Rules

Walmart's standard return policy is **90 days** for most items. However, electronics like laptops, tablets, and TVs have a much shorter **15-day** window, and major appliances have only **2 days** for damage reports.

The "No Receipt" Pro Tip

If you lost your receipt, you can still return items. Walmart allows up to three returns without a receipt within a 45-day period by scanning your government-issued ID. The refund will usually be placed on a Walmart gift card.
