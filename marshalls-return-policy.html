How Marshalls Returns Work

Marshalls has a 30-day return window for most items. Receipts after 30 days usually get store credit, and online orders can have a longer return window depending on the purchase date.

Holiday extensions are typically posted in-store and online each season. Keep an eye on the printed deadline for gifts.
