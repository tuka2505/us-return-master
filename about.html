About US Return Master

Welcome to US Return Master, your premier destination for navigating the complex world of retail return policies. Founded in 2026, our mission is to empower consumers with accurate, real-time data to ensure they never miss a refund deadline again.

We understand that every retailer—from Amazon to Walmart—has its own set of rules, exceptions, and holiday extensions. Our team of shopping enthusiasts and data analysts meticulously tracks these changes so you don't have to. Whether you're returning a defective gadget to Best Buy or a pair of shoes to Nike, we provide the clarity you need to shop with confidence.
