The 30-Day Window

Walgreens accepts returns for most items within **30 days** of purchase. To get a full refund, items must be in original condition with the receipt or a digital version from your MyWalgreens account.

Returning Without a Receipt

If you lose your receipt, you can still return items for store credit at the lowest advertised price. You must present a valid government-issued photo ID for all returns made without a physical or digital receipt.
