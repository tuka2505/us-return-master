Label-Free, Box-Free Returns

Amazon has partnered with retailers like **Kohl?™s, Whole Foods Market, and The UPS Store** to make returns incredibly easy. You don't need a shipping box or to print a label. Just bring your item and the QR code from your Amazon app.

How to Generate Your QR Code

1. Go to 'Your Orders' and select 'Return or Replace Items'.
2. Choose the reason for return and select the 'Drop-off' option that specifies 'No Box or Label Needed'.
