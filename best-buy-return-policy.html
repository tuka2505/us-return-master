Mastering Tech Returns

Best Buy's standard return window is 14 days. This is shorter than many other retailers, so it's critical to test your new gadgets immediately. Items must be in "like-new" condition with all original packaging.

Avoid Restocking Fees

Be aware that activatable devices (like smartphones) often incur a $45 restocking fee if opened. Major appliances also have a strict 15-day return window. Members of My Best Buy Plus/Total often enjoy an extended 60-day window.
