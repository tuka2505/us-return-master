Major Retailer Holiday Rules

Most major US retailers like Amazon, Walmart, and Target offer extended return windows for the holiday season. Items purchased during the peak shopping months are typically eligible for return until **January 31st of the following year**, providing extra peace of mind for gift-givers.

Crucial Exceptions: Electronics & Tech

Electronics and Apple products often have shorter holiday extensions, usually 15 days from Dec 25th or until mid-January. Always use our calculator to verify your specific item's exact date and avoid missing the deadline.
