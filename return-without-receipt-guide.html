Common Ways to Verify Your Purchase

Most major retailers have modernized their systems to help customers who lost their receipts. In 2026, you can typically use your **ID, the credit card used for purchase, or the store's official app** to track down your transaction and process a return.

The "ID for Store Credit" Rule

If no record can be found, many stores like Walmart or Home Depot may still offer a return in exchange for a **Store Merchandise Credit**. You will need to provide a government-issued ID, and the refund will be for the item's current lowest selling price.
