The Money Back Guarantee

Even if a seller says "No Returns," the eBay Money Back Guarantee protects you if the item is not as described. You typically have 30 days from the delivery date to file a claim.

Who Pays Shipping?

If the item is faulty, the seller pays. If you just changed your mind, check the listing to see if the seller offers free returns or if you must pay the return shipping fee.
