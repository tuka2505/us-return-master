The 180-Day Advantage

Kohl's offers one of the most generous return windows in the department store category: **180 days** from the date of purchase. This applies to most items, including clothes and home goods.

Amazon Returns at Kohl's

Kohl's is a primary partner for **Amazon returns**. You can bring your Amazon items to any Kohl's without a box or label, and they will pack and ship it for you for free. Note: Sephora at Kohl's items follow Sephora's stricter 60-day policy.
