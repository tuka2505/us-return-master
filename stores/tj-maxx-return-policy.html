Store vs. Online Policy

Items purchased in a TJ Maxx store must be returned within **30 days**. For online orders, you have a slightly longer window of **40 days** from the date of your order.

Return In-Store to Save

Returning an online order by mail incurs an **$11.99 shipping and handling fee**. To keep your full refund, simply take your online purchase to any local TJ Maxx store for a **free return**.
