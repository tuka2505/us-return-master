Old Navy 2026 Return Basics

Old Navy keeps returns straightforward. Most items can be returned within 30 days from purchase with proof of purchase. The standard window covers everyday clothing, kids apparel, and accessories.

Helpful Return Tips

Bring your receipt or digital order confirmation for the fastest refund. For gifts, in-store credit is commonly issued when the original payment method is not available.
