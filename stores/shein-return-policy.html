The 35-Day Window

Shein allows you to return items within 35 days of the purchase date. The first return from every order is free, but subsequent returns from the same order will incur a $7.99 shipping fee.

Instant Refund Trick

If you want your money back fast, choose to have your refund sent to your Shein Wallet. These refunds are usually processed within 24 hours of the return being scanned, whereas original payment methods can take up to 10 days.
