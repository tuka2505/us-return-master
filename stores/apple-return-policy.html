The 14-Day Rule

Apple maintains a strict 14-day return policy from the date you receive the item. This applies to all hardware, including iPhones, Macs, and iPads. The item must be in its original condition with all accessories included.

In-Store vs. Online

For the fastest refund, visit an Apple Store. Your refund will be initiated immediately, though your bank may take 3-5 days to show it. Online returns via mail can take up to 2 weeks for processing.
