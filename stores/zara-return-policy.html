The 30-Day Limit

Zara items must be returned within 30 days of the shipment date. The window is strict, so ensure you track your package delivery date carefully.

In-Store is Always Free

Returns made at any Zara store in the same country are free. However, if you choose to return by mail, a $4.95 fee will be deducted from your refund amount.
