The 90-Day Policy

Temu allows you to return items within **90 days** of the purchase date. Their first return for **every single order** is free, providing a risk-free shopping experience for new users.

Temu Credit vs. Original Refund

If you choose **Temu Credit** for your refund, it is processed almost instantly once your return package is scanned at the drop-off point. Refunds to original payment methods (Credit Card, PayPal) can take up to 18 business days.
