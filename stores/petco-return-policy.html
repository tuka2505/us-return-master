The 30-Day Policy

Petco's standard return window is **30 days** for most items. Items must be in their original packaging and accompanied by a valid receipt or your Vital Care membership info.

Live Animal & Food Rules

Live fish and small animals cannot be returned. However, if your pet refuses to eat a new brand of food, Petco typically allows you to return the **opened bag** within 30 days as long as it's at least half full.
