The 30-Day Window

Lululemon accepts returns within 30 days of the delivery date. Unlike Nike, items must be in original condition, unwashed, and unworn, with the original tags still attached.

Final Sale Exclusions

Items purchased from the 'We Made Too Much' section are final sale and cannot be returned. However, if your item has a manufacturing defect, their Quality Promise may cover a replacement even after 30 days.
