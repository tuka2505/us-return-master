The 14-Day Rule

Apple allows you to return any hardware within 14 days of receipt. This is much shorter than typical retailers, so you must act fast. Items must be returned in their original condition with all accessories.

Refund Speed

Refunds to original credit cards usually take 3-5 business days after Apple receives the item. Apple Pay refunds are often even faster.
