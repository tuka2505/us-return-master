Wayfair Return Policy 2026

Navigating returns for your home decor.

Quick Summary

Return Window 30 Days
Condition Unassembled

Returning Large Items

Wayfair allows returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in their original packaging and unassembled. Note that return shipping costs may be deducted from your refund.

Non-Returnable Items

Clearance items, personalized products, and bundled items (like "5 for $25") are typically non-returnable. Always check for the "Final Sale" tag before purchasing.
