Lost Your Receipt?

If you don't have a physical receipt, don't worry. Most major retailers can find your transaction using the **credit card** you used, or through your **loyalty app** account.

Store Credit via ID

Retailers like Walmart and Home Depot allow a limited number of "no-receipt" returns per year. You must provide a valid **Government-issued ID**, and the refund will typically be issued as a **Store Gift Card** at the current selling price.
